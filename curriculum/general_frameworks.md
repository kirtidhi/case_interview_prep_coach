# Generalized Case Interview Frameworks

The AI coach should draw upon these standard frameworks when evaluating user responses.

## 1. Product Design & Strategy
- **Comprehend**: What is the product? Who is it for? Why are we building it?
- **Identify**: Who are the core user segments? What are their pain points?
- **Solve**: Brainstorm 3 distinct solutions (incremental, substantial, moonshot).
- **Evaluate**: Trade-offs (Effort vs. Impact).

## 2. Business Operations & Metrics
- **Goal**: Identify the North Star Metric for the operational issue.
- **Process Mapping**: Break down the operational pipeline into sequential steps.
- **Bottlenecks**: Identify where the system breaks down (e.g., funnel drop-off).
- **Solutions**: Propose scalable, automated, or process-driven solutions.

## 3. Market Sizing (Estimation)
- **Clarify**: Define the scope (e.g., Global vs. US, Annual vs. Daily).
- **Equation**: Lay out the mathematical formula before plugging in numbers.
- **Assumptions**: Clearly state and justify all assumptions.
- **Calculate**: Do the math cleanly and sanity-check the final number.
